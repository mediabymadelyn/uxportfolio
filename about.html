ABOUT

I'm Madelyn (Maddi). I design things, build things, and think a lot about both.

WHO I AM

I grew up making things with my mom, a teacher who was always doing creative projects with me and her students. That turned into a love of drawing, then digital art, then animation and anime. Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, and anime were a huge part of how I developed my visual taste. As the internet evolved , I evolved with it, learning to draw digitally, watching art communities grow and shift, and seeing AI reshape the creative landscape in real time. I wanted to be someone building thoughtfully in that space, not just an active consumer of it.

Computer science came from a different but parallel love of games, technology, and functionality. I never had to choose between the two. Studying both CS and studio art at Occidental gave me the chance to incorporate both into my projects.
